Competition Guidelines

  • Eligibility

    Open to SCS freshmen & sophomores only. Teams of 3–5 members. One SDG track per team.

  • Targeted

    Pitches must answer the specific Urgency Question for your chosen SDG. Vague awareness pitches are disqualified.

  • Context

    Solutions must include a local use case — applied to a specific community or industry in your province.

Challenge Tracks

SDG 1 · SDG 4 · SDG 8
Smart Pathways to Inclusive Education and Livelihood
Empower marginalized communities through education, skills development, and sustainable employment.
Q1 How can we build an inclusive financial tool that allows the unbanked to weather economic shocks without falling back into extreme poverty?
Q2 How can we use AI or low-bandwidth tech to ensure foundational literacy for every student, regardless of school infrastructure?
Q3 How can we create a digital bridge that connects unskilled youth to high-demand "green jobs" with fair-wage protections?
SDG 2 · SDG 3
Technology for Nutrition Security and Community Health
Improve access to nutritious food, healthy lifestyles, and stronger public health systems.
Q1 What agricultural technology can double the productivity of small-scale farmers while reducing post-harvest waste by 50%?
Q2 How can we deploy decentralized diagnostic tools to ensure rural communities receive essential care within 30 minutes?
SDG 11 · SDG 13
Resilient Smart Cities for a Changing Climate
Design innovations that make cities safer, greener, and climate-adaptive.
Q1 How can we retrofit urban infrastructure or informal settlements to protect residents from climate-related disasters like extreme flooding or heatwaves?
SDG 14 · SDG 15
Protecting Biodiversity Through Technology and Innovation
Preserve ecosystems, protect wildlife, and promote environmental sustainability.
Q1 How can we use real-time monitoring to help local fishing communities identify and prevent illegal (IUU) fishing?
Q2 What scalable reforestation solution can incentivize private landowners to restore biodiversity while generating income?
